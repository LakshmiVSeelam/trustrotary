Major Achievements of Rotary Club of Kakinada Central since 1982

  • In 1987, a free ENT, Skin, Gynic and Dental camp was conducted at cost of 1,50,000/-. More than 700 Patients benefited during the camp.
  • In 1989, Physiotherapy equipment was provided to Government General Hospital Kakinada at the cost of 3,72,000/-
  • In 1996, a Family Planning Operations for females was conducted at a cost of 1,00,000/-. More than 360 operations were done.
  • In 1997, Rolls Royce Tennis Ball Night Cricket Tournament was conducted for 24 days in Association with Rolls Royce co., at a cost of 6,00,000/-.
  • In 2002, an Obstetric ICU was provided to Government General Hospital Kakinada at a cost of 8,33,000/-.
  • In 2003, an Eye Camp was conducted at the cost of 1,88,000/-
  • In 2005, a Mega Medical Health Camp was held at Sampara at the cost of 1,00,000/- with an objective to provide Health checkup to all the patients attending the camp. (More than 2000 patients attended). Hundred cataract operations were done during the camp.
  • In 2007, a Mega Eye camp at Tuni in Association with walkers club was conducted.
  • Between 2006 & 2009 more than 150 students who left Municipal Schools, joined “Rotary Bridge School” which was financially supported by our club members & Sarva Shiksha Abhiyan.
  • In 2007, the Physiotherapy unit at General Hospital Kakinada was upgraded at a cost of 6,09,000.
  • In 2009, a Gynic ICU unit was provided to Government General Hospital Kakinada at a cost of 8,11,000/-.
  • In 2007, a Vocational Training School was established to empower women in the community at a cost of 50,000/-.
  • In 2011, 1064 Educational books were donated to a library at Cameron Elementary and Junior High School, Texas, USA at a cost of 7,l0, 000/-.
  • In 2011, a Neonatal unit was provided in the Paediatric department of Government General Hospital Kakinada at a cost of 13,93,000/-.
  • In 2014, more than 1000 English - Telugu Dictionaries were distributed to the economically poor students of Municipal Schools in and around Kakinada at a cost of 1,00,000/-.
  • Between 2014 & 2016 TEN R.O water plants were provided to different villages & schools in and around kakinada at a cost of 19, 80,000/-.
  • In 2016, more than 3600 pairs of slippers were distributed to Municipal schools students of Kakinada with an objective to provide comfort to their feet at a cost of 1, 95,000/-.
  • In 2016, free Eye check up camps was conducted in 14 Municipal schools of kakinada in Association with Kiran Eye Hospital Kakinada at a cost of 2,00,000/-. More than 5000 students benefited. 250 specticals were distributed, 40 cataract operations, and 4 retina related surgeries were done to the needy students.
  • In 2016, As a part of Multi Rotary District singing competition, a Mega singing event “Reliance Rotary The Golden Voice” was organised for the youths of Kakinada. More than 100 boys and girls participated.
  • In 2019, for the first time our club conducted Rotary District Conference at a cost of 1,00,00,000/-. More than 700 Rotarians & Guests participated.

Apart from all these service activities our Club Members have together contributed more than
1,00,00,000/- (One Crore) to The Rotary Foundation.
