Our logo is a reflection of our Vision and Mission

  • The number 16 resonates with wisdom, independence, and family. 16 is a number of introspection. It is wise, intuitive, and tends to be independent. Hence the 16 spokes
  • Blood globally represents life itself, as the element of divine life that functions within the human body closely tied with passion, but also with death, war & sacrifice. Hence the drop of blood emoji
  • Physiotherapy encompasses rehabilitation, injury prevention and health promotion. It's the spine, or backbone of active and healthy life. More often than not physiotherapy helps prevent major Surgeries. Hence the human with backbone emoji
  • The hand that cares is holier than the lips that pray. Skill development is a vital tool to empower people, to safeguard their future and for the overall development of an individual and it needs motherly care for improving expertise in different fields of knowledge. Hence the hand emoji.
